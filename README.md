# **Plain English Summary**

We built CrushIt for families. Before the legal language, here's what we want you to know in simple terms:

- We do NOT sell your data - ever.
- We do NOT show ads to children.
- We CANNOT read your family's tasks, kids' names, or private information - your data belongs to you.
- Your family's data is isolated from every other family. No family can see another family's data.
- You can delete your entire account and all associated data at any time, instantly.
- We comply with COPPA (U.S. children's privacy law) and GDPR (European privacy law).

The full legal policy follows. If you have any questions, email us at <privacy@crushitapp.com>.

# **2\. Who This Policy Applies To**

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of CrushIt, including:

- **Parents/Guardians** ("Parent Users") - adults who create and manage a family account
- **Children** ("Child Users") - minors added to the app by a Parent User

CrushIt is designed for family use. Child accounts can only be created by a verified Parent User. Children may not independently create accounts or provide personal information without parental consent.

# **3\. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)**

CrushIt takes the privacy of children extremely seriously and complies fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and its implementing regulations (16 C.F.R. Part 312).

## **3.1 Parental Consent**

We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13. A Parent User must:

- Create the family account using their own email address and identity
- Explicitly create child profiles within the app
- Agree to this Privacy Policy on behalf of their children

By creating a child profile in CrushIt, the Parent User confirms they are the parent or legal guardian of that child and consents to the collection of information as described in this policy.

## **3.2 What We Collect from Child Profiles**

For child accounts, we collect only the minimum information necessary:

- Display name (chosen by the parent - does not need to be the child's real name)
- Username (for login - chosen by the parent)
- Encrypted PIN or password (hashed - never stored in readable form)
- Optional avatar photo (uploaded by parent or child with parent supervision)
- Optional emoji avatar (selected in-app)
- Task completion records within the family account
- Point balances and reward redemption history within the family account

We do NOT collect from children: full legal name, date of birth (beyond optional age range for level display), school information, location data, contacts, or any behavioral data used for advertising.

## **3.3 Parental Rights Under COPPA**

At any time, a Parent User may:

- Review the personal information we have collected about their child
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of their child's account and all associated data
- Refuse to permit further collection or use of their child's information

To exercise these rights, contact us at **<privacy@crushitapp.com>** or use the Delete Account feature in Settings > Account > Delete Family Account.

- **Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to provide the CrushIt service you signed up for

- Rectification - correct inaccurate personal data

- Restriction - limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
- Objection - object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, email **<privacy@crushitapp.com>**. We will respond within 30 days.

## **4.3 Data Retention**

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

# **6\. How We Use Your Information**

We use your information only to:

We NEVER use your information to: serve targeted advertising, build advertising profiles, sell to third parties, train AI models, or conduct behavioral research without explicit consent.

# **7\. Family Data Isolation & Our Access**

## **7.1 Your Family's Data is Isolated**

Each family's data is stored in completely isolated database records. Row Level Security (RLS) policies are enforced at the database level, meaning:

- Family A's data cannot be accessed by Family B - not by accident, not by design
- A child account cannot access parent-only features or other families' data
- Isolation is enforced by the database engine itself, not just application code

## **7.2 Our Access to Your Data**

CrushIt staff have extremely limited access to production user data:

- No employee can browse family task lists, kids' names, or reward histories in the normal course of their work
- Database access requires multi-factor authentication and is logged and audited
- Any access to user data for support purposes requires a support ticket and is logged
- We do not access your data unless you explicitly request support that requires it

We are working toward a zero-knowledge architecture in a future update, where even our infrastructure cannot read user content in plaintext. We will announce this update in our privacy changelog.

# **8\. How We Share Information**

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

## **8.1 Service Providers**

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate CrushIt:

- **Supabase (database & authentication):** Stores encrypted user data. SOC 2 Type II certified. Data processed in the US.
- **Apple Push Notification Service / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging:** Delivers push notifications. We share only the device token, not message content.
- **Apple Sign In / Google Sign In:** Optional authentication. We receive only a unique identifier and optionally your name/email. Governed by Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

All service providers are bound by data processing agreements that prohibit them from using your data for any purpose other than providing the service.

## **8.2 Legal Requirements**

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of CrushIt, our users, or the public.

## **8.3 Business Transfers**

If CrushIt is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and in-app notice before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. You will have the option to delete your account before any transfer.

# **9\. Data Security**

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- Passwords and PINs are hashed using bcrypt - we never store them in readable form
- Database access is protected by Row Level Security enforced at the database layer
- Multi-factor authentication is required for all administrative access
- Photo proofs are stored in encrypted object storage with signed URL access
- Signed photo URLs expire after 24 hours
- All access to production data is logged and audited

No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

# **10\. Data Retention & Deletion**

## **10.1 Active Accounts**

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to allow you to reactivate your account.

## **10.2 Account Deletion**

You can delete your family account at any time from Settings > Account > Delete Family Account. Upon deletion:

- All family member profiles are permanently deleted
- All tasks, rewards, points history, photos, and activity logs are permanently deleted
- Deletion is complete within 30 days
- Backups containing your data are purged within 90 days

This action is irreversible. We do not retain ghost records or re-identifiable backups beyond this period.

## **10.3 Individual Child Account Deletion**

A Parent User can delete an individual child's profile from the Kids management screen. This permanently deletes all data associated with that child profile.
